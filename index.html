How to Play

The goal is to make lines, horizontally or vertically, of three or more matching dinosaurs. Swap two adjacent pieces, and if you make a line of three or more pieces, it will disappear and new pieces will fall onto the board. The more matches you make, the higher your score. Hit the X T I N C T I O N button for a new board if you run out of moves
